Welcome to CNY Gym Centre of DeWitt and Clay, NY!

We offer gymnastics classes for boys and girls ages 6 months to 18 years. We coach competitive gymnastics team levels 2 - 10 and Xcel. CNY Gym Centre also offers Funtastic birthday parties, school vacation and summer camps,ninja classes, back handspring and tumbling clinics. We offer month-to-month tuition, providing convenient scheduling. A safe gymnastics environment is our priority, with team coaches who are trained and accredited, with background checks, and ongoing training for certification. Our staff includes knowledgeable ex-gymnasts. Be sure to check out our Ninja Warrior Training, gymnastics-influenced training for boys and girls, offered only at our ShoppingTown location.

Mission Statement

At CNY Gym Centre, our mission is to help each gymnast reach their full potential as a gymnast and as a person. We treat each athlete as an individual doing our best to foster confidence and lasting self-esteem. We strive to provide a safe and quality gymnastics program that will have an important and positive impact in your childâ€™s future. We believe that gymnastics is a vehicle in which we can teach skills that will last a lifetime, well beyond their gymnastics years.

Vision

With over 30 years of experience, we've become one of the top gymnastics clubs in New York state. We offer programs for children 6 months-18 years. Our programs range from Baby Gym and Parent and Tot to our birthday parties, summer camps, and our high performing teams. Whatever you sign up for, you can always expect that students will be taught in a fun, safe, and positive environment. Here, your child will gain new skills, learn life lessons, make great friends, and become a part of a close community like no other.
