At CNY Gym Centre our experienced staff are focused on providing your child with a unique camp experience that he or she will remember forever. Boys and girls of all gymnastics backgrounds and skill levels will have the chance to develop new skills, make friends and above all have fun!

Camps fill up quickly! To register or for more information call us at our Shoppingtown Location, (315) 437-4535, or Great Northern Location - (315) 622-4600
