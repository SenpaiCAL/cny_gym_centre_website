Check out our Youth Camps!


At CNY Gym Centre our experienced staff member are focused on providing your child with a unique camp experience that he or she will remember forever. Boys and girls of all gymnastics backgrounds and skill levels will have the chance to develop new skills, make friends, and above all have fun!

We have our Summer Fun Camps and School Vacation Camps to keep your kids active when school is not in session! Don't miss out on the FUN! Sign up TODAY!

Camps are offered at both our Shoppingtown Mall and Great Northern Mall Locations!
